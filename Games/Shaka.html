ABOUT THE GAME

Forge Your Own Legend

Shaka's Adventure is an open-world RPG inspired by the landscapes, cultures, wildlife and history of Southern Africa.

Explore villages, cities, sacred sites, battlefields, forests and untamed wilderness while building alliances, mastering combat and shaping the future of entire kingdoms.

Every decision matters. Every encounter leaves a mark. Every journey becomes a story.

FEATURES

Unexpected Encounters Await

Rescue travelers from dangerous wildlife, uncover forgotten ruins, settle disputes between rival clans, investigate mysterious legends and influence major events throughout the world.

Every choice affects your reputation, alliances, rivalries and the fate of the people around you.

COMBAT

Master The Warrior's Path

Train under legendary warriors and develop your own fighting style through skill, discipline and experience.

  • Short Spears
  • Long Spears
  • Throwing Spears
  • Shield Combat
  • Knobkerries
  • Mounted Warfare
  • Battlefield Formations
OPEN WORLD

Explore A Living World

Journey through forests, rivers, mountains, villages, cities, sacred sites, hidden caves, battlefields and remote wilderness.

NPCs live daily lives, follow schedules, travel between settlements and react to events.

TRAVEL

Ride Across The Kingdom

Travel quickly across the continent using a variety of unique mounts.

  • Horses
  • Cattle
  • Oxen
  • Lions
  • Elephants
  • Legendary Mounts
FACTIONS

Shape The Balance Of Power

Build relationships with kingdoms, clans, merchants, explorers, warrior regiments and foreign powers.

Become a trusted ally, feared enemy or respected leader.

SETTLEMENTS

Villages And Great Cities

Discover handcrafted settlements filled with stories, opportunities, traders, warriors and hidden secrets.

WILDLIFE

Hunt, Track And Survive

Encounter lions, elephants, buffalo, crocodiles, rhinos, leopards and many other creatures across the world.

CHOICES

Ten Distinct Endings

Become a king, conqueror, explorer, protector, merchant prince, diplomat, beastmaster, warlord, exile or legend.

Your actions determine how history remembers Shaka.
